Step 4

Step 5

Proceed to complete the payment process

Check Internet Connection

Make sure you have active internet connection.

Locate

Locate the charging port on the scooter, placement.

Plug

Take the charging gun and plug it into the charging port in the scooter.

Display

The screen will display “connected” & the light on the charger will go from Solid Blue to Blinking Green.

VIDA App

Goto the VIDA App, click the Charge Now icon on the screen

Status

The TFT screen will now turn “Charging” from “Connected” & the light on the charger will go from Blinking Green to Solid Blue.

End Charging

When you decide to end the charging process, you can click End charging on your app, complete the payment through the options available

Disconnect

Your VIDA V1 is now charging. You can check the state of charge either on the TFT Screen or on your app while is connected to your VIDA V1
